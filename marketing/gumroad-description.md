## Full Description (copy-paste into Gumroad editor)

**Ace your next tech interview with AI in your corner — and no one will ever know.**

PrepAura is a lightweight desktop app (Mac + Windows) that listens to your interview questions and instantly gives you AI-powered answers with code examples. It runs in a floating overlay that is **completely invisible to all screen sharing and recording software**.

---

### ✦ What it does

- **Listens to your interview** — captures microphone + system audio so it hears both you and the interviewer
- **Gives instant AI answers** — powered by Llama 3.3 70B (via Groq), answers stream in under 2 seconds
- **Shows 5 progressive approaches** — from basic concept to advanced pattern to edge cases and gotchas, just like a senior engineer would answer
- **Captures coding problems** — screenshot a HackerRank or CoderPad problem and get a full solution
- **Stays invisible** — excluded from Zoom, Google Meet, Teams, and browser-based screen sharing at the OS level
